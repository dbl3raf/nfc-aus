Nocturnal Flight Call Library

This library documents nocturnal flight calls recorded over Brisbane as part of an ongoing effort to characterise the nocturnal soundscape of Australian birds. Because many Australian species have never previously been studied in this context, identifications are assigned an evidence level reflecting the strength of supporting information.

Evidence levels

Confirmed
The call has been directly linked to the species through unequivocal evidence, such as a photographed or visually observed bird producing the sound.

Strong
The identification is very strongly supported by acoustic similarity to curated reference recordings and by ecological context, with no plausible alternative species.

Probable
Evidence suggests the call belongs to the proposed species, but some uncertainty remains, for example due to limited reference recordings or possible confusion species.

Tentative
A working hypothesis based on limited evidence. The call resembles the proposed species but additional data are needed to confirm the identification.

Unresolved
A distinct call type has been recognised but the species producing it is currently unknown.
