Buff-banded Rail SQUEAK

The SQUEAK call is described in HANZAB as a harsh, creaky preep, usually repeated 4–5 times at intervals of 15–20 seconds. The nocturnal flight call appears to match this daytime vocalisation closely, both in sound and in the spacing between calls.
