Dusky Moorhen

Gallinula tenebrosa

Dusky Moorhen Strongly supported

Putative nocturnal call type

Variety of Dusky Moorhen calls, still awaiting classification into call types. One important trick will be to separate out flying birds and ensure these are given a particular call type. Would be good to find recordings from dry sites with no moorhens in earshot!

Evidence for identification

Comparison with knowledge of daytime calls. Would be worth making a definitive spectrogram graphic.

Confidence statement

Strong, but there are many borderline calls that are hard to be sure about; best to classify these as unknown.

Similar species / confusion risks

Brief calls that sound like Dusky Moorhen could come from a wide variety of other species, so care is needed.
