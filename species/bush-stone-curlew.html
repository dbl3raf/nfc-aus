Putative nocturnal call type

Variety of Bush Stone-curlew calls, still awaiting classification into call types. Although this is a resident species in many areas, migration is not out of the question, so definitive records of flying birds are of interest.

Confidence statement

High confidence since this is a well-known vocalisation.

Similar species / confusion risks
